Happy Swipe

A simple and fun photo and video viewer, perfect for young children.

Features:

  • simple up/down swipe action to move between photos
  • read-only access - no way to accidentally change or delete photos
  • tap the clock to fill the border with a different color
  • choose a random emoji to add to each photo
  • rate each picture with 1-5 hearts
  • colors and emojis are saved for each photo but the original photo is unchanged
  • album can be selected in settings menu which is accessed by holding down the settings icon (hard for kids to accidentally access!)
